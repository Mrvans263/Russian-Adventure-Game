📜 HISTORICAL CONTEXT
Pavilion №1 (Central Pavilion) is the architectural heart of VDNKh — the Exhibition of Achievements of the National Economy. The current building was reconstructed between 1951 and 1954, after Stalin's death, and features a 97‑meter golden spire crowned with a red star. The pavilion was designed to showcase the economic and industrial success of the Soviet Union, with grand halls representing each of the 15 republics. Its monumental Stalinist Empire style — massive columns, sculptures of workers, and opulent interiors — was intended to impress both Soviet citizens and foreign visitors with the supposed abundance and power of socialism.
🇷🇺 Павильон №1 — центральное здание ВДНХ. Построен в 1951–1954 годах. 97‑метровый шпиль с красной звездой — символ сталинской архитектуры. Здание демонстрировало успехи советской экономики и должно было внушать гордость гражданам и уважение иностранцам.
